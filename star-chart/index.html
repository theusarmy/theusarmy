What is this?

To be promoted to Elder, you need to get at least 10 total stars in war. You can see how many stars you have in total by looking at the table below.

Note that we count all your war stars, not just your new war stars. So if you score 3 stars on a base that had previously been 2-starred, you will be awarded the full 3 stars, not just the 1 new star.

Note: Stars from wars before 6/11/17 are not counted, because that is when this webpage was created.

Protip: to make it easier for you to find your name, entries are in alphabetical order (so for example, "Fury" would come before "loones").
