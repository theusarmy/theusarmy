When do we war?

We war whenever enough members request a war in clan chat. Usually, this happens once or twice a week.

Do I need to use both attacks?

Yes, you always need to use both attacks. There are no exceptions to this rule. Below are the consequences.

Participation Amount Consequence
0 attacks Demotion, or kick.
1 attack Demotion, or warning.

Getting demoted or kicked out will reset your war star progress. This means you will have to get 10 more war stars to get Elder back.

Do I have to be in war?

No. Opting into war is encouraged, but not mandatory. However, the only way to get Elder is to score 10 or more total war stars.

However, if you do not want to be included in wars, make sure to opt-out of war(set your shield to red). Otherwise, you will face the full consequences of not attacking, regardless of what your excuse is.

We let you make the choice, so choose wisely!

What's the reward for participating in war?

We keep track of every star you get, and once you get a total of 10 or more, you get Elder.
