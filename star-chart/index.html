What is this?

To be promoted to Elder, you need to get at least 10 total stars in war. We keep track of them here. Note that you only need to get 10 total stars, not 10 new stars in war. So if you get three stars on a base that was previously two-starred, then you get awarded the full three stars, not just the one new star.
