How do I get promoted to Elder?

At the end of every war, we count how many stars you contributed, and we add it to your star count (click here to view how many stars you've gotten). Once you have 10 or more stars, you are eligible for promotion. This doesn't guarantee you'll be immediately be promoted, but you are eligible to become an Elder, and you will likely be promoted soon. However, keep in mind there are other factors that determine how fast you get promoted. In order of most significant to least significant, they are:

  1. War Contribution (10 star minimum)
  2. Clan chat activity. Speak up! It shows that you're active.
  3. Donations

Can I get promoted to Co-leader?

To be honest, we are not planning to promote anybody to Co-leader anytime soon, though it is still a possibility.

Why did I get demoted?

If you've been demoted, you've either been inactive (no donations, no receptions, no chat messages, and no war opt-in) for a long time, or have opted into war, and only used one or zero attacks. Don't worry! If you use both attacks in the next few wars, you'll be re-premoted in no time!

I forgot to opt-out and got kicked. That's not fair!

Don't worry! We all make mistakes, which is why we always invite members back once they have been kicked (unless they have done something flagrantly offensive). Once you are back from your trip, or are no longer super busy, then you can start playing CoC again and be welcomed back into our clan.
