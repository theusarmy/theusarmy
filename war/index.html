When do we war?

We war whenever enough members request a war in clan chat. Usually, this happens once or twice a week.

What are the consequences of particpating or not participating in war?

Well, that depends on how much you participate. See the table below.

Participation Amount Consequence
0 attacks Demotion, or kick.
1 attack Demotion, or warning.
2 attacks Congratulations, you're one step closer to a promotion!

Do I have to be in war?

No. Opting into war is encouraged, but not mandatory.

If you opt-out (make your war preference shield red)...

... you will not be put in wars. If you are accidentally added, you will be exempted from the normal consequences of inactivity. However, the only way to get promoted is to participate in wars, and we encourage you to opt-in whenever possible.

But if you do choose to opt-in (make your war preference shield green)...

... you will be expected to use both attacks to the best of your ability. If you don't, then you will face the full consequences.

We give you the ability to choose whether you opt-in or opt-out...

... so use it! If you don't want to fight in a war and you get kicked for inactivity, you only have yourself to blame for not opting out.
